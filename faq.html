Q2 : Why does the order arrive later than the time you have been informed by call center?
A2 : There are many factors that can make the order arrive late including road traffic conditions, the number of orders the store has, weather conditions and the accident on the road.
Q3 : Can I pay by cheque?
A3 : Sorry, we do not accept any cheques but you can use EFTPOS card and cash to pay when your order is delivered.
Q4 : Can I use Dream Pizza member card for delivered order?
A4 : Yes, you can but just only collect the point. You can not use it for discount.
Q5 : How Dream Pizza calculate a point from order price when customer use Dream Pizza member card?
A5 : We calculate $0.25 per 1 point.
Q6 : What is the benefits of Dream Pizza point?
A6 : It depends on promotion each time, Sometimes you earn Dream Pizza voucher up to the overseas plane ticket.
Q7 : How much it costs when customer want to extend the membership?
A7 : It costs you $10 per one-year membership.
Q8 : Can the customer extend the membership more than one year?
A8 : Yes, you can. It costs $10 per one-year membership.
Q9 : How much discount that the customer can get when they use member card?
A9 : The customer can get 10% discount from total cost when they use with dine-in or pick-up order but delivered order.
