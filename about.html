About Us

Our first Dream Pizza restaurant in New Zealand was opened in New Lynn, Auckland in February 1970. Currently, we have 80+ branches for the entire country.

Our company have been growing very fast over past 45 years. We expanded average 80+ branches for each ten years. We know what Kiwis like, then we discovered the recipe of pizza that can make Kiwis love it and come back to order it very often.

We are still maintaining our principles to make our customer pleasurely and keep developing new pizza menus for our customers every week. We are also keeping improve our fault in our service to make our customer more comfortable to use our services.

Our Pizza's Standard

Deep Pan
Our Pan Pizza is New Zealands favorite! The thick crust is baked to golden perfection, crispy on the outside, soft and chewy on the inside.
Thin and Crispy
Our thinner crust baked perfectly so you can taste more of your favorite toppings.
Perfecto
A pizzeria style crust not too thick and not too thin.
Original Stuffed Crust
The extra playful pizza you eat backwards! Pizzeria-style crust with a ring of melted cheese baked right into the crust.
Gluten Free
Our gluten free base is made from rice flour and maize starch and is available only in a large base.
